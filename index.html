Rural Areas of Pakistan

Rural areas are villages and countryside regions where most people depend on agriculture or livestock for their livelihood. They offer open space, cleaner air, and close-knit communities.

Urban Areas of Pakistan

Urban areas are cities and towns with dense populations, advanced infrastructure, and diverse economic activities. These regions offer better access to education, healthcare, and public services.

Desert Areas of Pakistan

Desert areas are dry regions with very little rainfall and extreme temperatures. Despite the harsh climate, these regions have unique ecosystems and communities that thrive through adaptation and resilience.
